About Cashix

Cashix is a well-known name in the world of payment apps, serving over 100 million users across the country. Over the years, Cashix has transformed to become one of the leading financial services and investment apps in India. From recharge & bill payments to investment & lending, Cashix lets you do it all effortlessly. On the lending front, the offerings span across Buy Now Pay Later, Personal loans, Credit Cards, all supported by Axis Bank.
